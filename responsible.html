Responsible Gaming

At Dude Spin, we are committed to promoting responsible gaming and ensuring a safe and enjoyable gaming experience for all our players. We recognize that while gaming can be entertaining, it can also become problematic for some individuals.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to:

  • Preventing underage gambling
  • Protecting vulnerable players
  • Providing tools and resources for responsible gaming
  • Offering support to players who may have a gambling problem

Age Restrictions

You must be at least 18 years old (or the legal age of majority in your jurisdiction) to use our Service. We verify the age of all players and reserve the right to request proof of age at any time.

Signs of Problem Gambling

Gambling should be fun and entertaining. If you recognize any of these signs, you may have a gambling problem:

  • Spending more time or money than you can afford
  • Chasing losses or gambling to recover losses
  • Neglecting work, family, or other responsibilities
  • Borrowing money to gamble
  • Feeling anxious or depressed about gambling
  • Lying to family or friends about gambling
  • Gambling to escape problems or negative emotions

Self-Exclusion Tools

We provide several tools to help you control your gambling:

  • Deposit Limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits
  • Time Limits: Set time limits for gaming sessions
  • Loss Limits: Set limits on losses within a specified period
  • Reality Checks: Receive reminders about how long you've been playing
  • Self-Exclusion: Temporarily or permanently exclude yourself from the Service

To access these tools, please contact our support team or visit your account settings.

Cool-Off Periods

If you feel you need a break from gaming, you can request a cool-off period ranging from 24 hours to 6 weeks. During this time, you will not be able to access your account or place bets.

Permanent Self-Exclusion

If you decide you need to permanently exclude yourself from our Service, we will close your account and prevent you from creating a new account. This decision is final and cannot be reversed.

Help and Support

If you need help:

If you believe you have a gambling problem, please seek help immediately. There are many organizations that can provide support and assistance:

BeGambleAware.org Gambling Therapy GamCare Gamblers Anonymous

You can also contact our support team for assistance with self-exclusion or accessing responsible gaming tools.

Protecting Minors

We take the protection of minors seriously. We do not allow anyone under the age of 18 to use our Service. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Our Policies

We have implemented strict policies and procedures to:

  • Verify the age and identity of all players
  • Monitor for signs of problem gambling
  • Intervene when necessary to protect players
  • Provide resources and support for responsible gaming

Contact Us

If you have any questions about responsible gaming or need assistance, please contact our support team. We are here to help.

Remember: Gambling should be fun and entertaining. Never gamble more than you can afford to lose. If gambling stops being fun, it's time to stop.
